Rule methodology, equations, and observation dates

Inputs

  • it-1: lagged effective federal funds rate.
  • r*: neutral real rate, using either the SEP longer-run measure or the Holston-Laubach-Williams estimate.
  • π and π*: PCE inflation and its target; πe is the SPF forecast.
  • u gap: the workbook unemployment-gap measure.
  • gN and g*: nominal GDP growth and its target; N gap is the nominal-spending gap.

How to read the dashboard

The monetary rules translate inflation, labor-market conditions, nominal spending, the neutral real rate, and prior policy settings into federal funds rate prescriptions. They are benchmarks for evaluating policy—not mechanical instructions or predictions.
